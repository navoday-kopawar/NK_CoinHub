About Us

We are two friends who love collecting coins and banknotes! What started as a fun hobby has now become a growing digital collection. Through this website, we share our favourite finds, detailed information, and interesting facts about each currency piece. If you’re a collector — or just curious — we hope you enjoy exploring our collection!

Navoday Atul Kopawar

I am passionate about numismatics and love discovering the stories behind every coin and banknote. I enjoy organizing the collection, researching historical details, and building this website to present our work beautifully.

Komal Gajanan Wankhede

I have a deep interest in rare currency and unique historical pieces. I focus on collecting, cataloging, and helping expand the variety of items in our growing collection.
